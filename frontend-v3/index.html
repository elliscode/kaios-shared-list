      FAQ

      • How do I share a list with someone? Open the list you want to share and press the left softkey (labelled "Share"). You can then send the link by text or email. Anyone who opens the link will have the list added to their app automatically.
      • What do you do with my email? We only use your email to send you a one-time sign-in code. We don't store your email address in our database — once the code is sent, we don't keep a copy of it.
      • Where is my data stored? Your lists are stored securely on Amazon Web Services (AWS), a trusted cloud platform used by millions of apps and businesses worldwide.
      • How long is my data kept? Your data is automatically deleted after 1 year of inactivity on your account.
      • What other data do you collect? We store the IP address each request came from, a random device identifier, and some technical headers provided by AWS. This is standard for any online service and is used only for security and troubleshooting.
      • Do you sell my data? No. We do not sell your data, and we never will. This is a personal hobby project — if it ever becomes too expensive to run, we will simply shut it down rather than sell your information.
      • What if the person I'm sharing with has a different kind of phone? No problem! The shared list link opens in any web browser, so your friend can use it on an iPhone, Android, or computer — they don't need a KaiOS device.
      • Who can see my lists? Only you and anyone you choose to share the link with. The developers cannot read your list contents — all data is encrypted in the database.
